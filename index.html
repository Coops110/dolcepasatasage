French & Italian
Excellence

Dolce Pasta is more than a restaurant; it is a celebration of culinary heritage. By marrying the delicate techniques of French cuisine with the robust, comforting flavours of Italy, we create an experience that speaks to the connoisseur in everyone.

Our lounge offers a serene escape, featuring a curated selection of fine wines and artisanal pasta dishes prepared with the freshest seasonal ingredients. Every detail, from the ambient lighting to the velvet textures, is designed to evoke a sense of quiet luxury.
